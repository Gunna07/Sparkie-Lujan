THE PARENTS CALLING OF A CHILD

Where is my child that I gave birth to
She now flies with God, and the angels too
I miss her smile at the break of dawn
Her tangled long hair, her soft stretching yawn

Her calling out daddy on dark stormy nights
My visions of us playing in the park as we use to fly kites
Lace in her dress, and her pigtails all curly
Dancing about the morning hours so early

How could our baby, be gone so soon
She was just here yesterday, as the clock struck noon
Didn't that man know, that drinking can kill
This feeling inside is making me ill

What is the sense in all of this pain
Show me the lesson that parents of dead children can gain
The laws of the road, seem to have no foundation
Now as my child rests, in Gods holy creation

The man who was drinking had no regard for her life
He himself has left behind three children and a wife
Wonder if he knew that three drinks were to many
When in reality drinking and driving with one, is more than plenty

So many lives will suffer due to his lack of regard
Burning pain inside with flames that can only cause char
Emotions of anger and rage are now housed in me
Please follow the rules of the land, as they can saves lives you see!

(Dedicated to Casey, with love Sparkie)
