How It Is

Tell me how it is so I can understand
Show me how it works so I can expand
Teach me slowly the ways of the world
Talk to me, when life throws me into a whirl

Days and nights, clamoring into one pool
Making my life, useful, creative, and a valued tool
Guide me so I am able to share, what life has shown
Making me, stable, worldly, and mentally grown

Growing up in days with so much emotional distress
Has made my mental images, confused and a mess
Hold my hand, when the light is no longer bright
Tell me again, how to make things for both of us right

Give me directions, so that I can walk to you straight
Making me whole, and pure, for the white pearly gate
Strengthen me with convictions, of what purity means
Making me tougher and wiser, like an old pair of blue jeans

Make life with me, gentle as we walk in the dark
Show me again, the beauty, that's hidden in some snow laden park
Smiles on my face, coping with life's hurried old pace
Loving you in the end, is how I'd wish to win this race

Step by step, we can share the wisdom we have obtained
Making each of us whole and enlightened, once again
Lights shining brightly, as we gaze in one another eyes
Hearing the "I love you", as we hold one another in replies

Taking my heart, as nature had intended us to do
Life is sharing the knowledge and love, as we always knew
Growing closer, and holding one in the grasp of love
How nicely we fit together, like a warm leather glove
Sparkling in Gold...
