Children's Inspection

Cherry trees blooming in the hot summer days
Low laying clouds, fog, and man made haze
How can we breath into our lungs, air with drippings of dirt
The companies doing this, sit, smile, and smirk

Our children, have futures at stake, as we once did
How can they expect us not to clean up the grit
Rules, and regulations, and voting on unheard issues
As the children breath, cough, and cry...as they hold tissues

It is time, to take a stance and make a commitment this day
Stand together, clean up the earth, and salvage what we made
Make this earth safe for our children to rejoice in
Once this is accomplished, all humanity will win

Breathing clean air, is not a God given right
What it is, is something we have to have in our eye sight
So pull together, and work in the same direction
Making clean air our priority, for our children's inspection
