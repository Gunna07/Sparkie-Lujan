How Can We Let The Children Cry

How can we let the children of the world cry ?
Sitting in our snuggled warm beds, with a dry eye
Letting so many children go hungry each full day
Filling their small tummies, is what I pray

Never hearing their screams of pain, as the night gets darker
How come us humans, keep missing the marker
Getting food to the children so they can survive
If we keep missing the marker, thousands will die

Life is for living the best possible way
How can we let the children starve in hunger each day
Give to the children, a small part of your means
Creating in us a world full of peace and lovely things

Don't let the children's tummies feel the hunger pain
Think how much all of our futures can gain
Please give of yourself to the children today and you will find
Happiness in your heart, and great peace of mind!!

(Dedicated to all the babies, and their hungry tummies)
